A little About Me

I currently attend the University of Guelph and have completed 2 years thus far. As a student, I am still constantly learning new things, and have a real interest in learning more. Languages


The language that I am most familiar with is C. Now I understand that most modern software doesn't really involve C, however, I believe that learning C was very beneficial because of how low level it is. It has given me a deeper understand of why things are the way they are, and it also made me a better programmer because of the fact that C doesn't hold your hand. I learned how to manage memory and pointers, and due to the little support C provides, I am able to complete tasks without the need for extended libraries or built in features.

Next to C, the language I am the most familiar with is Java. I completed a university course covering this language, and feel that my knowledge in C was incredibly useful when it came to learning Java. I also have a little experince with JavaScript, jQuery, HTML, CSS, and MySQL. While practicing with these languages, I integrated C code with Javascript code, used HTML, CSS and jQuery for the GUI, and utilized MySQL to save the data needed for the dynamic webpage.

I have experience with Unix/Linux, and MacOS, as these are the Operating systems I use the most. I am also familiar with Windows, but as I use a mac now, my strength is with Apple's OS. I recently have also experimented with Python, gaining an understanding of the basics.
